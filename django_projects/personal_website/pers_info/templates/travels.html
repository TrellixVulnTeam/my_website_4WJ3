Travels

    Over the winter break of 2018 I travelled to a few countries in Europe. It was my first time stepping into Europe, so everything seemed novel to me. While it opened my eyes to the advanced societies and infrastructures built in Europe, it also got me thinking how differences in culture, history, and governance shaped each of the cities.

    Although I travelled mostly on a budget, I managed to cover 4 countries over 10 days comfortably.
  • <Munich, Germany>

  • <Salzburg, Austria>

  • <Copenhagen, Denmark>

  • <London, UK>

    Coincidentally, I was reading "Empress Dowager Cixi: The Concubine who Launched Modern China" right before arriving in the UK. Juxtaposing the societies of China and the UK today (China has an even longer history than the UK), it struck me how a weak government can unravel all the nation's past glory and destroy its identity/culture.
